As you enter the Web3 gaming space, it's critical to prioritize security because blockchain technology brings unique challenges and opportunities:

1. **[Security Fundamentals](/docs/trust-safety)**: Enjin's tools robust security for all in-game transactions, ensuring that your economy is protected from fraud. This security instills trust and promotes sustained engagement, as players feel more comfortable engaging with your game in the longer term.

2. **[Immutable Asset Ownership](/docs/immutable-asset-ownership)**: The permanence of transactions in Web3 games, where items, once distributed to a player, cannot be revoked, poses significant challenges for developers and necessitates stringent measures:

3. **[Real-Value Transactions](https://enjin.readme.io/docs/real-value-transactions)**: The games often involve real-value transactions. Secure management of these transactions is imperative to prevent theft or loss due to hacks or fraud.

4. **[Anonymity and Privacy](https://enjin.readme.io/docs/anonymity-and-privacy)**: The privacy of players in Web3 games is a critical concern, especially when cryptocurrency wallets can be linked to real-world identities.

5. **[Scalability](/docs/scalability)**: Security and privacy foundations must be robust to scale safely as the game grows and becomes a more prominent target for attacks.
